Shipping

Please allow 24-72 hours for processing after we receive your order (excluding Saturdays). All orders are subject to processing time, which is distinct from the time it takes for a shipment to arrive at its destination after it has been fulfilled. You will receive a tracking number as soon as the processing is completed.

Returns

We have a 30-day return policy, which means you can request a return within 30 days of receiving your item. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging, and it must be in the same condition that you received it. You will also require a receipt or proof of purchase.

Refunds

We will notify you once we have received and inspected your return and will let you know whether or not the refund was approved.
